CLASS
Ketemmu (Dormant Memmu)
ALIASES
The God in Pandora’s Box
ORIGIN
Contained in Pandora’s Box, age unknown; first observed through a temporal window by ███████ researchers.
PHYSICAL DESCRIPTION
8-dimensional entity; appearance cannot be fully perceived by human senses; partially manifests as shifting geometric patterns in quantum space.
BEHAVIOR
Dormant most of the time; inevitable Rapture event will occur upon containment breach or natural decay of Pandora’s Box.
EFFECTS ON HUMANS
Cognitohazard: all religions and cultural beliefs indirectly influenced by TA-39203’s interference; severe psychological impact if observed directly.
CONTAINMENT PROCEDURES
Quantum containment; multiple layers of reality dampeners; permanent containment attempted, no known failsafe exists.
NOTES
Eventual Rapture is inevitable; observers under influence of TA-32093-B’s quantum shield may survive initial effects.

First recorded in ███████ urban alleyways; manifests in dense fog.
